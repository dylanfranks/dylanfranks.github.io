Description

Deadman's Dark Scenery Court takes place in a haunted mansion you have broken into during the night. You must solve puzzles to venture deeper into the mansion.

Hints to puzzle solutions can often be found written on the walls. Hints and solutions can also be found in the readme. The gameplay is inspired by old point and click adventure games by Sierra Entertainment.

History

Deadman's Dark Scenery Court started out as a collaboration between Dylan Franks and Filip Skwarski. Dylan was inspired after playing Filip's game Loneliness, and Filip allowed Dylan to use the “Loneliness” engine for his own purposes.
