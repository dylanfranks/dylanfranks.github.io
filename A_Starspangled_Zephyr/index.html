Description

A Starspangled Zephyr is an experimental arcade game. You drift through space and fight psychedelic boss battles, who strike in erratic, amorphous patterns.

The visuals are dark, but also bright and colorful. The gameplay is accompanied by ambient synthesizer music, which adds an ominous tone to the game.

History

A Starspangled Zephyr was a collaboration between Dylan Franks and Travis Schutter. Dylan built the original prototype and sent it to Travis. They began bouncing engines back and forth, until Travis produced a superior engine.

Dylan built upon this and added most of the game's content - most notably the music, and the psychedelic bosses. A Starspangled Zephyr was first released in April 2010.
