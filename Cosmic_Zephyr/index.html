Description

Cosmic Zephyr is an arcade/action game for Windows and Android. You chase your enemy at high speeds, over a randomly generated path in outer space.

You chase one enemy in each level, and must shoot them down without being taken out yourself. The game demands fast reflexes to stay on track while attacking the enemy.

The soundtrack is heavily inspired by Sonic R, and consists of seven pieces composed by Dylan, including one vocal track that serves as the main theme.

History

Dylan began developing this game in 2010. He added most of the final touches in summer 2013. He has since updated it several times - most notably localizing it to 6 other languages.

Cosmic Zephyr was influenced by the bullet hell genre which was popular at the time, and sought to capture the effect in 3d. The game was also inspired by various racing games like Extreme G, Jet Moto, AeroGauge, Wave Race 64, and Burnout 3.

Features

  • Combo system
  • Automatic save / load system
  • Five levels over different planets and galaxies
  • Downloadable soundtrack with over 22 minutes of music
  • Available in 7 languages (English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, and Russian)
